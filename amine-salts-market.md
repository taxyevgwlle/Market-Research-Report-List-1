<p><ul><li>Diamine</li><li>Ternary Amine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Amine salts are categorized based on their structure and chemical properties, primarily into diamines and ternary amines. Diamine salts contain two amino groups, making them useful in diverse applications like polymers and surfactants due to their ability to enhance chemical bonding. Ternary amines, on the other hand, feature three organic groups attached to a nitrogen atom, contributing to their unique properties, such as solubility and reactivity, which are advantageous in pharmaceuticals and agricultural chemicals.</p></p>
