<p><p>Variable Reluctance Stepper Motors (VRSM) are electromechanical devices that convert electrical energy into precise mechanical movement. They operate based on the principle of varying reluctance between magnetic fields and a rotor with no permanent magnets. VRSM are known for their reliability, simplicity, and ability to provide high torque at low speeds, making them suitable for various applications in robotics, industrial automation, and medical devices.</p><p>The Variable Reluctance Stepper Motors Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.6% during the forecast period, driven by the increasing demand for automation across different industries. Additionally, the growing trend of miniaturization in electronic devices is increasing the adoption of compact and efficient stepper motors.
