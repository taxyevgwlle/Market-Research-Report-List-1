Staple fiber pads are composed of short individual fibers, providing flexibility and excellent absorption, making them suitable for various food applications. In contrast, long fiber pads consist of continuous strands, offering enhanced strength and durability, ideal for thicker or heavier food products. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining food freshness and safety by absorbing excess moisture and preventing spoilage.</p></p>
