<p><ul><li>Active Power Electric Power Meters</li><li>Reactive Power Electric Power Meters</li><li>Apparent Power Electric Power Meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric power meters market encompasses three main types: active power electric power meters, which measure the real power consumed by devices; reactive power electric power meters, used to monitor power that does not perform work but is necessary for maintaining voltage levels in the grid; and apparent power electric power meters, which measure the total power in a circuit, including both active and reactive components. Each type plays a crucial role in energy management, billing, and grid stability.</p></p>
