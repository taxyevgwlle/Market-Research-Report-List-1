<p><p>Airflow management equipment encompasses various products designed to optimize airflow in data centers and other facilities. Blanking panels fill unused rack spaces to prevent airflow disruption, while grommets seal cable openings, enhancing thermal efficiency. Air-filled kits create barriers against unwanted airflow. Enhanced brush covers improve sealing around cables. Air diverters manage airflow direction, while containment solutions isolate hot and cold air streams. High-flow doors facilitate airflow while restricting environmental exchange. Additional components may include specialized enclosures and airflow monitoring devices.</p></p>
