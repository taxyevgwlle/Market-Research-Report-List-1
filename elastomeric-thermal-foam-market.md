<p><p>The elastomeric thermal foam market is segmented into various types based on the polymer composition. NBR (Nitrile Butadiene Rubber) based foams offer excellent oil and fuel resistance, making them suitable for automotive applications. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) based foams are known for their weather resistance and flexibility, ideal for roofing and insulation. Chloroprene-based foams provide good chemical resistance and are commonly used in refrigeration. The "Others" category encompasses various specialty foams tailored for specific industrial needs.</p></p>
