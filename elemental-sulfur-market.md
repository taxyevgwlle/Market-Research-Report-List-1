<p><p>The elemental sulfur market includes granulated sulfur, powdered sulfur, and other forms. Granulated sulfur consists of small, solid pellets, ideal for agricultural and industrial applications due to its ease of handling and slow-release properties. Powdered sulfur, finely ground, is used in chemical manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, and fungicides for its rapid solubility. Other forms include liquid sulfur and prilled sulfur, catering to specialized industrial needs. Each type serves distinct purposes, driven by factors like particle size, solubility, and application-specific requirements.</p></p>
