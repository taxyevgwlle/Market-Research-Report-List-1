<p><ul><li>Beam Shaping (Top-Hat)</li><li>Beam Splitting</li><li>Beam Foci</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diffractive elements market encompasses various types, including beam shaping, beam splitting, and beam focusing. Beam shaping, notably in top-hat profiles, transforms incoming light into uniform distributions for applications like laser engraving. Beam splitting involves dividing light into multiple paths, crucial for experiments and optical systems. Beam focusing concentrates light into small spots, enhancing precision in laser machining and optical communications. Each type caters to specific applications, driving advancements in photonics and optical technologies across multiple industries.</p></p>
