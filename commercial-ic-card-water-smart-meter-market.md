<p><p>The commercial IC card water smart meter market comprises two main types: single-phase and three-phase meters. Single-phase meters are typically used for residential and small commercial applications, measuring low to moderate water consumption efficiently. In contrast, three-phase meters cater to larger industrial and commercial setups, handling higher water volumes and providing more robust data management. Both types facilitate prepayment through IC card technology, promoting better cash flow and enhanced monitoring of water usage in various commercial sectors.</p></p>
