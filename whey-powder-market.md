<p><p>Whey powder is primarily available in two forms: concentrate and isolate. Whey protein concentrate typically contains 70-80% protein, retaining more nutrients and bioactive compounds, making it suitable for general supplementation and food applications. Conversely, whey protein isolate is processed to contain over 90% protein, with lower fat and lactose content, making it ideal for athletes and those seeking high-quality protein in a purer form. Both forms cater to specific dietary needs and preferences, influencing their market dynamics.</p></p>
