<p><p>The pet parasiticide market is divided into two main types: internal and external parasites. Internal parasites include organisms such as worms and protozoa that inhabit the gastrointestinal tract, requiring treatments like dewormers. External parasites encompass pests like fleas, ticks, and mites that affect a pet’s skin and coat, treated with topical solutions, collars, or systemic medications. Both segments are essential for maintaining pet health, influencing product development, marketing strategies, and consumer preferences within the veterinary sector.</p></p>
