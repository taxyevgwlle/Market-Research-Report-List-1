<p><p>The organic canola oil market primarily consists of two types: pressed and leached. Pressed organic canola oil is extracted through mechanical pressing, retaining more nutrients and natural flavors, making it a preferred choice for health-conscious consumers. In contrast, leached organic canola oil undergoes a chemical extraction process using solvents, which can create a more refined product with a higher yield but may lose some nutritional value. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and applications in cooking and food production.</p></p>
