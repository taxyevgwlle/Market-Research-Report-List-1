<p><p>The cable ladder system market is primarily divided into two types: metal cable ladders and fiberglass reinforced plastic (FRP) cable ladders. Metal cable ladders are known for their durability, strength, and resistance to harsh environmental conditions, making them ideal for heavy-load applications in industrial sectors. In contrast, FRP cable ladders offer excellent corrosion resistance, lightweight properties, and lower thermal conductivity, making them suitable for corrosive environments, such as chemical plants and offshore facilities, where weight and resilience are critical.</p></p>
