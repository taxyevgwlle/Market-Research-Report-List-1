<p><p>The steam jet ejector market is primarily categorized into single-stage and multi-stage ejectors. Single-stage steam jet ejectors operate efficiently for low vacuum applications, utilizing steam to create a high-velocity jet that entrains gases. Conversely, multi-stage steam jet ejectors consist of multiple stages to achieve higher vacuum levels and improved efficiency in industrial applications. These ejectors find usage in processes such as vacuum distillation, drying, and gas extraction, catering to varying operational pressures and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
