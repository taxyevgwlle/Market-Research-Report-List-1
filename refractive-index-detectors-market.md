<p><p>Refractive Index Detectors (RID) are vital in chromatography, particularly in High-performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) and Size Exclusion Chromatography (SEC). In HPLC, RIDs help assess sample concentrations across various compounds, ensuring accurate quantification, especially for non-UV-absorbing substances. In SEC, they measure changes in refractive index as samples elute, providing insights into molecular size and distribution. Both markets benefit from advances in sensitivity and technology, enhancing analytical capabilities for industries like pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and food.</p></p>
