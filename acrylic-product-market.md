<p><p>The acrylic product market primarily includes **cast acrylic sheets** and **extruded acrylic sheets**. Cast acrylic sheets are manufactured by pouring liquid acrylic into molds, resulting in superior optical clarity, thickness consistency, and durability, making them ideal for high-end applications like signage and displays. Extruded acrylic sheets are produced by pushing molten acrylic through rollers, offering cost efficiency, uniform thickness, and ease of fabrication, commonly used in lighting, retail fixtures, and protective barriers. Both types cater to diverse industries, balancing quality and affordability.</p></p>
