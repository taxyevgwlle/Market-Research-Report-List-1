<p><p>Electric heating radiant ceiling panels are efficient heating systems integrated into ceilings, providing warmth through infrared radiation. The market can be divided into two main types: top-mounted and wall-mounted panels. Top-mounted panels are installed directly on ceilings, allowing for even heat distribution and freeing up wall space. Wall-mounted panels, on the other hand, are affixed to walls, making them suitable for smaller rooms or where ceiling installation is impractical. Both options enhance comfort while maintaining aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
