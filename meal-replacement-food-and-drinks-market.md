<p><p>The meal replacement food and drinks market is categorized into three main types: Ready to Eat (RTE), Ready to Heat (RTH), and Ready to Cook (RTC). RTE products are fully prepared meals that require no preparation, ideal for convenience. RTH options need minimal heating for consumption, offering a quick meal solution. RTC products require cooking but come with pre-measured ingredients and instructions, appealing to those who enjoy cooking while saving time on meal prep. Each type caters to different consumer needs for convenience and flexibility.</p></p>
