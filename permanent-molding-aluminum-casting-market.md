<p><strong>The Permanent Molding Aluminum Casting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity</li><li>Slush</li><li>Low-pressure</li><li>Vacuum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Permanent Molding Aluminum Casting market encompasses various techniques for producing aluminum components. Gravity casting relies on gravitational force to fill molds, ensuring good detail and surface finish. Slush casting involves partially filling the mold, allowing the outer layer to solidify, creating hollow objects. Low-pressure casting uses controlled pressure to inject aluminum into molds, enhancing density and detail. Vacuum casting enables the removal of air pockets during filling, producing high-quality, intricate parts with minimal defects. Each method serves specific industrial needs.</p></p>
