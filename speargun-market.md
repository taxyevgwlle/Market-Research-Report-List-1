<p><strong>The Speargun Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rubber (or band) Powered</li><li>Air Powered (Pneumatic)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The speargun market is primarily divided into two types: rubber (band) powered and air powered (pneumatic) spearguns. Rubber powered guns utilize elastic bands for propulsion, offering simplicity and reliability, making them popular among recreational divers. In contrast, pneumatic spearguns use compressed air to propel the spear, providing more power and range, appealing to experienced spear fishermen. Both types cater to different preferences and skill levels, influencing performance and usability in various underwater environments.</p></p>
