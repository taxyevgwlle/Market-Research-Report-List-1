<p><p>The semiconductor test socket market comprises various types, including BGA (Ball Grid Array), QFP (Quad Flat Package), QFN (Quad Flat No-lead), LGA (Land Grid Array), and SOIC (Small Outline Integrated Circuit). Each type serves specific packaging technologies, enabling efficient electrical connection for testing semiconductor devices. BGA sockets provide high-density connections, QFP offers flat package convenience, QFN is known for its compactness without leads, LGA utilizes land pads for connection, and SOIC features a small outline for space efficiency. Other types encompass emerging and niche packaging solutions.</p></p>
