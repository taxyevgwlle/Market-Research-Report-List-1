<p><p>The vertical laminar flow hoods market is categorized primarily by filter types: HEPA (High-Efficiency Particulate Air) and ULPA (Ultra-Low Penetration Air) filters. HEPA filters capture particles down to 0.3 microns with an efficiency of 99.97%, making them suitable for most laboratory applications. ULPA filters offer even higher efficiency, capturing 99.999% of particles down to 0.1 microns, which is ideal for environments requiring stringent contamination control. Both filter types enhance air quality by providing clean, unidirectional airflow essential for various applications in laboratories and cleanrooms.</p></p>
