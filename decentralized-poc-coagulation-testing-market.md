Anti-Coagulated Plasma Technology enables effective monitoring of anticoagulation therapy using plasma samples. Whole Blood Specimen Testing Kits facilitate rapid testing at the site of care, providing immediate results. Satellite Anticoagulation Therapy integrates real-time monitoring and treatment, enhancing patient management in decentralized settings. Collectively, these technologies improve accessibility, speed, and accuracy in coagulation testing.</p></p>
